Frequently Asked Questions

How do I turn the black wallpaper off? My screen remains black even after uninstalling the app.

All you have to do is set another wallpaper, using the built-in method offered by Android (such as going in your Settings app and tapping Display and then Wallpaper), or by using any other wallpaper app (such as Tecdrop's own RGB Color Wallpaper app).
